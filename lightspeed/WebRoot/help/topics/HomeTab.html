Home Tab

This is your starting point after entering a Production.  The Home page welcomes you to the Production and displays information tailored towards you and your role on the Production.

 

On the left side, the Home page lists the date and your upcoming call time and location, if applicable.  Below that it displays information about the Production schedule start and end dates.

 

The right side of the screen displays Production status, notifications, and your preferences.  You can switch between each of those views using the mini-tab menu selections in the blue bar immediately below "Home".
