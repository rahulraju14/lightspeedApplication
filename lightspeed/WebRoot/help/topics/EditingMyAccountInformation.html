Editing My Account Information

Select the Edit button in the upper right corner to edit your account information.  You can edit the following fields:

 

·         E-mail address

·         First name

·         Last name

·         Gender

·         Ethnic Code

·         Citizenship

·         Date of Birth

·         Address

·         State of Residence

·         Minor and Agent status

·         Phone numbers

·         Emergency Contact information

·         Agent information

·         A link to your IMDB page

·         Loan-Out Corporation information

 

When complete, select Save in the lower right corner.

 

Uploading Pictures and Headshots

To add pictures to your account, select the Images mini-tab and click the Edit button.  Select the New Image button to add a new picture to your account.

 

Note: Pictures may not be larger than 6 MB.

 

Changing Your Password

You may change your password by clicking on the Change My Password link at the bottom of the My Account page.

 

Change My Password

Changing Your PIN

Directly below the option to change your password is the option to change your PIN. PINs are four digit codes and are used to confirm identity for electronic signatures.

 

When you change your PIN you will be asked to re-enter your Lightspeed password, as well as your new PIN.

Deleting Your Lightspeed Account

You may delete your Lightspeed account by clicking on the Delete My Account link at the bottom of the My Account page.

 

Warning: Deleting your account is permanent and cannot be reversed!  Do not delete your account unless you are certain you will never need to access your Lightspeed information again.
