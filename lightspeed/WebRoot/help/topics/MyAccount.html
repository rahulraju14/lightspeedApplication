My Account

The My Account tab is used to view and change your Lightspeed account information.  Use this screen to edit your contact details such as e-mail, address, and phone numbers, and upload headshots or images of yourself.  This screen is also where you may change your password or delete your account entirely.

 

Note: When you accept a production invitation you are presented with the option of transferring some of your contact details (address, phone, and pictures) into the production.  If you do not want to share these details, do not transfer them into the production.
