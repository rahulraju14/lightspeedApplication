Home

The Home screen, on the right, contains a several mini-tabs.  Use the Status mini-tab to view the latest information pertaining to you about the production.  Select the Notifications mini-tab to view all of your notifications.  The My Preferences mini-tab allows you to view and change your personal preferences regarding how Lightspeed will communicate with you.

Status

The Status mini-tab displays a summary of the current project status and your most recent notifications.  The information displayed in the Status area varies depending on your role in the production.  Some or all of the following data will be present:

 

·         Work Status:

Displays the start and wrap times for the most recent shooting day, and links to the last several daily Production Reports.  It includes any incidents from the most recent Production Report, and an estimate of overtime hours recently worked.

 

·         Recent Notifications:

Your four most recent notifications are displayed here.  Click on one to read it on your notifications page.

 

·         Materials Status:

This section displays a list of the production’s film stock and media inventory including the amounts purchased, used, and remaining.

 

·         Report Status:

The Report Status lists the reports for which you are responsible which have upcoming or overdue deadlines.

 

·         Element Status:

This section provides a list of the Script Element categories in the current script shows which categories have outstanding unfulfilled requirements.  For example, if your script contains 12 character roles and four of them have been cast, the Characters category will display a value of 4/12.

Notifications

My Notifications displays the messages sent by Lightspeed to your account.  The notifications screen consists of two parts – the notifications list and the message display.  Click on a notification to view the message in the message display.  Unread notifications appear in bold font in the notification list.

 

At the bottom of the notification list are pagination controls.  The notification list displays up to 50 notifications.  If you have more than 50 notifications use these controls to view additional pages of notifications.

 

To delete a notification, click the Delete button in the message display area.

 

A Sent Notifications mini-tab is visible to certain production roles, such as Producers, Directors, and Assistant Directors.  Use the Sent Notifications mini-tab to review the notifications that have been sent from Lightspeed.

My Preferences

This mini-tab allows you to subscribe to notifications and published documents from the production.  When you subscribe to a document you will receive a new copy of the document in your e-mail when the document is first published, and in some cases, when the document is updated.  Select the Edit button to make changes to these settings.  When done, select the Save button at the lower right corner of the screen.

 

Use the Deliver Notifications to section to select where you would like your notifications sent.  Notifications can be sent to e-mail addresses and cell phones.  If you have an assistant you may send the notifications to that individual’s e-mail or cell phone.

 

The Notification Subscription and Document Subscription sections offer you options to subscribe to various types of notifications.  The following types of subscriptions are available:

 

·         Call Sheet Changes

Receive a notification when a new Call Sheet is published, or when changes are made to the current Call Sheet.  Changes include such things as location, scene, or call time changes.

 

·         Calendar Changes

Receive a notification when the work days, travel days, or off days are changed on the production calendar.  Only changes affecting the next seven days generate a notification.

 

·         Task Reminders

If you’re responsible for creating Call Sheets or other reports, these notifications remind when the tasks are due or overdue.

 

·         Call Sheets (sent to your e-mail)

Receive a copy of the most up to date Call Sheet when it is published or changed.

 

·         Location Directions (sent to your e-mail)

Receive directions to the next day’s location(s).  These are delivered when a new Call Sheet is published.

 

·         Advance Script Pages (sent to your e-mail)

Receive copies of the scenes to be shot for a given production day.  Select how many days in advance you’d like receive them.

 

Don’t forget to select the Save button after making your changes.
