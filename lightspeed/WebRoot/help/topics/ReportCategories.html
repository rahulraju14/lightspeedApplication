Report Categories

Report types and categories are listed in the left pane.  The following categories are available:

 

·         Cast & Crew

·         Scene Breakdown & Shooting Schedule

·         Day out of Days (DooD) & Elements

·         Strip Board

·         Call Sheet

·         SAG Exhibit G

·         Production Report

 

Select a category from the list to display report options on the right.  In the case of daily reports such as Call Sheets, Exhibit Gs, and Production Reports, a list of report instances will appear on the right allowing you to view and edit specific daily instances.

 

Task Status

Below the report categories is the task status table.  This table provides a high level overview of the status of the daily call sheet, Exhibit G, and production report.  A red flag indicates that the report has not yet been started.  A Green check means the report is complete.  A Yellow pencil means the report is in progress.
