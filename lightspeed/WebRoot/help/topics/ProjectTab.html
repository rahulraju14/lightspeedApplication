Project Tab

The Project tab contains information pertaining to the entire production, and in the case of a TV series, individual episodes.  This information includes the following:

 

·         The Production Company name, address, and contact phone numbers

·         Preferences and Task Assignments for the production

·         Production staff members

·         Film stock and media inventory

·         User permissions within Lightspeed

 

Beneath the Project menu selection is a submenu containing three choices: Projects, Materials, and Permissions.  Each takes you to a separate page as described below.
