My Productions

This is your starting point after logging in to Lightspeed.  The My Productions page displays a list of your productions on the left and displays more detailed information for a specific production on the right.

 

My Productions

 

On the left side, the Production List shows the Title, Start Date, and Production Day (if applicable) for each of your productions.  If a production is episodic, such as a TV series, then the number of the most current episode appears after the Title.

 

Production List

 

Note: Click on the Create button at the top of the Production List to create a new production.

 

Episode numbers, start dates, and production days are entered and computed from with each production.  To change any of these values, enter the applicable production and make the appropriate changes to the episode list, calendar, or schedule.

 

Each production name is a link.  Click on a name to enter a particular production.  If you click on other areas of the row (such as the Start Date or Day area) then you will select that production's row without navigating into the production.

 

The right side of the screen displays the Production Title and information about the Production Company.

 

Production Details

 

If you have ownership or administrative rights for the production you will be able to view production subscription details.
