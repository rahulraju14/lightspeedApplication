Cast & Crew Tab

The Cast & Crew tab is used to access the list of people and departments associated with the production, also known as the Cast & Crew List.  Cast, crew, managers, and vendors can all be added to the Cast & Crew List.  Each person is assigned a role that identifies their position in the production.

 

Beneath the Cast & Crew menu selection is a submenu containing two choices: Cast & Crew List and Departments.  Each takes you to a separate page as described below.
