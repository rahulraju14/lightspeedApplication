Production Details

On the right side of the My Productions page is the production detail area.  Click on a production's row on the

 

This area contains the production title and the name and address of the production company.  The production's e-mail notification prefix is listed here as well.  This notification prefix is the sending e-mail address that will be used by the production to send e-mail notifications to cast and crew.  For example, if my_production@lightspeedeps.com is chosen, then your cast and crew will receive messages from that address.  Notifications@lightspeedeps.com is the default.

 

You may also add a logo for the production or production company.

Editing Production Details

Select the Edit button in the upper right corner of the screen to edit these fields.  When you are done select Save.
