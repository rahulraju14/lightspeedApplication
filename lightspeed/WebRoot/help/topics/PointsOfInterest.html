Points of Interest

Points of Interest List

Points of Interest (POI) are businesses and facilities near shooting locations that provide services useful to the production.  Photocopy centers, restaurants, parking facilities, and hospitals are examples of points of interest.  The POI list is the complete list of points of interest which have been input into the production.  The list displays each item’s name, phone number, and category.

 

There are six categories of points of interest.  You can filter the list using the category dropdown at the top of the list.

 

Creating a New Point of Interest

Use the following steps to create a new point of interest (POI):

 

1.      Select the Add button at the top of the points of interest list.

A new POI record is created and opened in the right pane.

 

2.      Enter information into the various POI fields.

 

3.      Select the Images mini-tab and add images, if applicable

 

4.      Select the Save button in the lower right to save your changes.

Point of Interest View

The right pane displays the details of a selected point of interest.

 

Viewing a Point of Interest

Select a point of interest from the list to view its details on the right.  The POI screen contains two mini-tabs: Details and Images.

Details

The Details mini-tab displays the information for the selected point of interest.  This information includes the address, phone number, and type of POI.

Images

The Images mini-tab displays images that have been uploaded for the POI.

 

Editing a Point of Interest

Select the Edit button in the upper right corner of the screen to edit the selected POI.  The POI screen switches to edit mode allowing you to edit the fields as needed.

 

Deleting a Point of Interest

Select the Delete button in the upper right corner of the screen to delete the selected point of interest.  The Delete Point of Interest popup window prompts you to confirm.  Click on the Delete button to delete the point of interest.

 

Warning: Once a point of interest has been deleted, it cannot be restored.
